WELCOME!

Hi there! We are Bas Oudejans and Lars Vermolen. We researched how photography has changed over the years, and what aspects make up for a good picture. On this website you can view the practical part of our research. We decided to visit the Castricum dunes and forest to make pictures. We focussed on the five key aspects of a good photo: light, point of view, composition, shape and ambiance. With these aspects, we tried creating perfect pictures. Decide for yourself if we succeeded by viewing our pictures below!

ABOUT THE RESEARCH


This website is our practical part of our big research for ACE. We wanted to do something with photography. As research question we chose: 'How did photography change over the years?' We answered this question with the use of three sub-questions: 'How did photography start?', 'How is photography now?' and 'How did the aspects of a good picture change?' For the practical part we decided to shoot lot's of pictures and create a website to show them, so they are accesible for everyone.
